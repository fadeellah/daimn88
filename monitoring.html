Sparing Monitoring Concept

The need to establish SPARING requires adequate facilities and ensures that the collected data serves as a reliable reference. The process begins with sample collection, which is then analyzed using sensors. The sensor data is sent to a Water Quality Datalogger, which meets the required standards for data acquisition in water quality monitoring.Next, the recorded data is transmitted to a central server, where it is stored and processed. Finally, the collected data is presented in the form of reports, providing valuable insights for monitoring and analysis.
